Adventures in Fire Island

Fire Island offered just the right dose of quiet and weird. Between barefoot walks on pine-needle trails and salt-wind naps by the lighthouse, it felt like a dream tucked between ocean and sky. We watched deer sneak past beach plum thickets, chatted with strangers on ferry decks, and let the sunsets rinse the day clean.
